Ben Thanh Art Gallery (BTA gallery) was found by Ms.Nguyen Thi Thu Suong in 1999 as a Contemporary Fine Art Gallery and house of frames.

Since Ben Thanh Art Gallery (BTA gallery) was founded, we have had the pleasure to serving the most deluxe hotels in Ho Chi Minh City as InterContinental Hanoi Landmark 72, The Grand Ho Tram Strip, Hyatt Regency Danang hotel & resort, Intercontinental Asiana Saigon hotel, Sheraton Nha Trang hotel & Spa, Park Hyatt Saigon hotel, Caravelle Saigon hotel, Princess D' Annam Phan Thiet and also oversea as Howard Johnson Hotel – Wuhan – China, Viethaus – Germany…,.. as well as many art collectors from all over the world.

We also have sponsored yearly exhibition for famous Vietnamese Artists in HCMC and oversea. Specially, Ben Thanh Art Gallery (BTA gallery) have held commercial Art auction with top artists of Vietnam and also assistant for Charity Art Auction of Operation Smile yearly since 2003, Heart Beat Vietnam 2010.

Ben Thanh Art Gallery (BTA gallery) is located at 7 Nguyen Thiep street, Dist 1, HCMC, Vietnam. The Ben Thanh Art Gallery (BTA gallery) currently established leading Vietnamese Artists and up coming artists of Bui Huu Hung, Tran Luu Hau, Hong Viet Dung, Dang Xuan Hoa,Dao Hai Phong, Dinh Quan, Nguyen Trung, Ho Huu Thu, Do Xuan Doan, Ha Huynh My, Pham Luc, Tran Dinh Khuong, Quan Tho, Nguyen Van Hai, Le Thi Anh Nguyet, Luc Dan My, Tra Giang, Doan Thuy Hanh, Tran Anh Huy, Dang Manh Ha, Vu Dung, Dinh Cong Khai, Dinh Ngoc Thang, Ho Huu Thu, Mai Xuan Oanh, Nguyen Dinh Quang, Nguyen Ngoc Anh, Pham Luc, Phan Mai Truc, Tran Dinh Khuong, Tran Thanh Canh, Than Trong Dung, Lieu Nguyen Huong Duong, Phan Hai Bang, Pham Huy Thong, Nguyen Than, Tran Quang Huan, Nguyen Thi Hoai Thuong, Nguyen Hai Anh and more...

As understanding of demand from art lovers all over the world, Ben Thanh Art Gallery (BTA gallery) has expanding another gallery middle of year 2006, Galerie Nguyen located on 139 Dong Khoi street, Dist 1, HCMC, Vietnam.

Main of the Gallery primary goals is to be a Art bridge for artists could reach to Art lovers in over the world.
